Business and IT Transformation

Business and IT Transformation (BITT) is the starting point for many of the use cases supported by Software AG´s Digital Business Platform. Covering the design and planning aspects of the digital transformation, the BITT solution is comprised of two market-leading products: ARIS and Alfabet.

Enterprise Architecure

Software AG's Alfabet Enterprise Architecture Management Platform provides a collaborative platform for aligning the IT landscape with the business to guide competitive transformation.

Portfolio Management

Software AG's Alfabet IT Portfolio Management Platform provides decision-makers with insight into the IT portfolio to make the right IT investment decisions and to plan how IT will support the business in the future.

Integration

Software AG's Integration Platform is a proven, pre-integrated software suite that enables businesses to rapidly integrate and manage systems, services, (mobile) devices, processes, business partners and data to provide new business value and improve business performance. 

Process

Software AG's Business Process Management (BPM) Platform gives businesses the ability to quickly locate issues, implement and change processes, and shift IT focus from maintenance to innovation. Process-driven solutions can be implemented by using the businesses’ existing IT landscape while automating processes and gaining end-to-end control and visibility.

In Memory

Software AG's In-Memory Data Fabric Platform is the first-choice platform for distributed in-memory data management, with extremely low predictable latency at any scale. The platform stores data in memory, where it is quickly retrievable by multiple users with multiple apps.
